Kannur Lokesh Rahul is an Indian international cricketer who plays for the Indian national team. A right-handed wicket-keeper batter, he plays for Karnataka in domestic cricket and he was the captain Lucknow Super Giants in the Indian Premier League from 2022 to 2024. Wikipedia
